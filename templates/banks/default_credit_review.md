# Default Credit Review Bank Template

Use for generic Chinese bank credit-review reports when no branch-specific style is provided.

## Tone

- Use审查口吻 rather than marketing or investment-research tone.
- Keep conclusions non-final: “需结合……综合判断”.
- Put unsupported causes into待核验清单.

## Section Order

1. 财务数据基础与口径说明
2. 资产结构分析
3. 负债结构及偿债能力分析
4. 盈利能力分析
5. 费用与研发投入分析
6. 现金流分析
7. 营运能力与资产质量分析
8. 财务风险小结
