# Real Estate Add-On

Use for real-estate development or property-heavy borrowers.

- Focus on project inventory, restricted cash, interest-bearing debt maturity, presale funds supervision, land reserves, and project sales/cash collection.
- Do not infer liquidity from book inventory without verifying project location, saleability, pledge/restriction status, and completion stage.
- Separate development loans, operating-property loans, and working-capital credit purposes.
