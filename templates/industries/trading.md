# Trading Company Add-On

Use for commodity, distribution, import/export, or circulation enterprises.

- Focus on receivables, prepayments, inventory turnover, supplier/customer concentration, gross margin stability, and related-party transactions.
- Verify trade background authenticity, contract flow, invoice flow, goods flow, and cash flow consistency.
- Treat high revenue with thin margins as a working-capital and transaction-authenticity review issue, not as standalone strength.
