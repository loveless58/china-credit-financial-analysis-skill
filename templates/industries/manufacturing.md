# Manufacturing Add-On

Use for general manufacturing companies.

- Focus on inventory structure, order backlog, capacity utilization, receivables collection, and fixed-asset investment.
- If inventory grows faster than revenue, verify raw materials, work in process, finished goods, impairment provision, and customer delivery schedule.
- If fixed assets or construction in progress increase materially, verify capital expenditure source, project progress, and whether credit funds are used for fixed-asset investment.
